Kuhli loaches are easy to overlook at first because they spend so much time at
the bottom and often move more at night. Once settled, they become a useful
and interesting cleanup fish for a calm aquarium.

The care sheet should point out the lid, the soft substrate, and the need for a
small group. Those are the practical details a keeper needs before bringing
them home.
