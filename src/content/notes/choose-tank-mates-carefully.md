  - Compare the temperature and tank size needs of every species you want to mix.

  - Add new fish slowly so the tank does not get overloaded at once.

Good tank mates are a matter of fit, not just looks. A fish that shares the same
temperature range but fights for space can still be a poor match.

Start with the basics: temperature, tank size, and temperament. If those line up,
then you can think about schooling habits, feeding style, and how active the fish
will be together.
