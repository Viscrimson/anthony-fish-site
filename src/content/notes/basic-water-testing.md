Water testing helps you catch problems before they turn into obvious stress for
the fish. Beginners do not need a complicated spreadsheet to start. A small
record of what was tested and when is often enough to show whether the tank is
settling or drifting.

Most beginners start with the basic checks that tell them how the tank is
behaving overall. Depending on the aquarium, other information such as pH or
temperature can help explain what is happening too.

The goal is not to chase every number. The goal is to understand the tank well
enough to keep it stable for the fish that live there.
