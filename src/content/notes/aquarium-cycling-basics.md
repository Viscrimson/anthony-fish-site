Cycling a new tank is the step that makes the rest of the setup work. It gives
the filter bacteria time to grow so the aquarium can handle waste more
reliably.

The exact method can change, but the core idea is the same: establish the tank
before stocking it heavily, keep the water stable, and test it regularly while
it matures.

Later articles can link to the specific care sheets that match the fish in the
system.
