Quarantine gives a new fish a calmer place to settle and gives you time to watch for
problems before they spread to the main tank. The setup does not need to be fancy;
it just needs to be stable, quiet, and easy to monitor.

The main idea is to keep the routine simple. Feed lightly, watch the fish closely, and
leave the water and equipment separate from the rest of the tank system until the fish
looks steady.
