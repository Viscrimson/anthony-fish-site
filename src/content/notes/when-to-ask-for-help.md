Most tank problems are easier to solve when the details are still fresh. Waiting too
long can make it harder to tell whether the issue was water, behavior, feeding, or a
mix of several things.

If you are not sure what is wrong, write down the facts first and ask for a second set
of eyes. A short note with the test results and behavior is often enough to point the
conversation in the right direction.
