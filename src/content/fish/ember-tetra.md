Ember tetras stay small, but they still need a calm environment and a real
school. They color up best when the tank is planted and the lighting is not too
harsh.

Use small foods that sink slowly or suspend in the water column so the school
can eat together without stress. Their small size makes them easy to overlook,
so the care sheet should keep the essentials compact and direct.
