White cloud mountain minnows are one of the easier small schoolers to keep if
the aquarium is cool enough. They are active, hardy, and well suited to a
quiet tank that is not heated for tropical fish.

The care sheet should make the temperature difference very clear so the wrong
setup is less likely. That helps the page stay practical for a quick QR scan.
