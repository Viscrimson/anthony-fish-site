Red wagtail platies are a straightforward fish for a small community tank.
They stay active in the open and do best when they can graze, feed, and move
without being crowded by aggressive species.

The care sheet should make the livebearer nature of the fish obvious. A
keeper should know the basics fast: easy food, peaceful tankmates, and the
chance of fry if mixed genders are kept together.
