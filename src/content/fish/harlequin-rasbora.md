Harlequin rasboras are one of the easiest fish to recommend for a calm
community tank. Their color and behavior show best when they can move together
in a group and do not have to compete with bigger species.

The care sheet should mention soft, stable water and a tank with plants and
open space. That gives visitors the practical setup they need without turning
the page into a breeding guide.
