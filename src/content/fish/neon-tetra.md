A neon tetra does best in a settled aquarium with gentle filtration and
consistent water quality. They show their best color when they have space to
school and enough cover to move through the tank without stress.

Feed tiny foods they can swallow easily and keep tankmates small and peaceful.
The care sheet should focus on water stability first, then a regular feeding
routine and a quiet group of fish.
