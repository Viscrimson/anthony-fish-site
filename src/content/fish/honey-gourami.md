Honey gouramis suit a quiet aquarium with plants, soft cover, and gentle
movement. They are not a fish to mix into a hectic tank with nippy or overly
active species.

This care sheet should help a keeper understand that the fish is small, calm,
and happiest when the surface of the aquarium feels safe and sheltered.
